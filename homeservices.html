Home Services Market Analysis (Pennsylvania)

Objective: Analyze competitive dynamics and pricing patterns in the PA home services market using Yelp data.

Problem

The home services market is fragmented and pricing transparency is limited. This project analyzes service density, rating distributions, and price variation to identify market inefficiencies.

Methodology

  • Data source: Yelp API
  • Data cleaning & transformation: Pandas
  • Visualization: Matplotlib / Seaborn
  • Statistical analysis: Python

Key Findings

1. Market Concentration

Service density is concentrated in metro regions, with significant whitespace in suburban markets.

2. Rating Distribution

Ratings cluster tightly around 4–4.5 stars, suggesting limited differentiation.

3. Price vs Rating

No strong linear relationship between price tier and rating — indicating pricing inefficiencies.

Business Implications

  • Opportunities in under-served suburban markets
  • Premium pricing not strongly correlated with quality signals
  • Room for platform-driven transparency improvements
